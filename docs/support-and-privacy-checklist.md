# Support And Privacy Checklist

Last updated: 2026-07-02

Before submission, confirm active public URLs for:

- Support page or support email
- Contact email
- Privacy policy
- Terms of service
- Installation or getting-started guide

Content requirements:

- Explain QST uses Shopify product data in read-only mode.
- Explain that QST Listing Workspace prepares export packs and does not publish live marketplace listings from the web dashboard.
- Explain that QST Desktop is optional.
- Explain optional eBay OAuth only as a QST Desktop/advanced local workflow, not a required Shopify app step.
- Explain how merchants clear browser-local QST edits and uninstall the app.
- Include Q-MER.CH contact details.

Do not include credentials, tokens, or private reviewer-only links in public policy pages.
