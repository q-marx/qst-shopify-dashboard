# QST Terms Of Service Draft

Publication note: this is product-specific draft copy for QST. Review with legal advice before publishing.

## Overview

QST Listing Workspace is a Shopify Admin app provided by Q-MER.CH. It helps merchants review Shopify product data, prepare marketplace listing drafts, and create export packs.

By using QST, you agree to use it only for lawful business purposes and in line with Shopify's terms, marketplace rules, and applicable laws.

## Shopify Read-Only Product Access

The Shopify Admin app uses read-only Shopify product access. QST Listing Workspace does not write product changes back to Shopify.

Local edits made in QST are for listing preparation and export workflows. They are separate from the merchant's Shopify catalogue unless the merchant manually uses exported content elsewhere.

## Marketplace Exports

QST export packs are preparation aids. The merchant is responsible for reviewing listing content, prices, images, policies, categories, shipping details, and marketplace compliance before importing or publishing content on any marketplace.

QST does not guarantee that any marketplace will accept a listing, category, image, policy, or export file.

## Optional QST Desktop

QST Desktop is an optional Windows companion for advanced local workflows. The Shopify Admin app remains usable without installing QST Desktop.

Desktop features may require separate local setup, marketplace authentication, and Windows permissions.

## Billing

For Shopify App Store users, subscription billing is handled through Shopify App Pricing or Shopify's billing systems. Q-MER.CH does not ask Shopify App Store users to pay for the Shopify app through an off-platform checkout.

## Acceptable Use

You must not use QST to:

- infringe intellectual property rights
- publish misleading, illegal, unsafe, or prohibited products
- bypass Shopify, eBay, or other marketplace rules
- attempt unauthorised access to systems or data
- misuse exported data or credentials

## Availability

QST is provided on an as-available basis. Q-MER.CH aims to keep the app reliable, but does not guarantee uninterrupted operation, marketplace availability, or compatibility with every store, product, account, browser, Windows device, or marketplace change.
